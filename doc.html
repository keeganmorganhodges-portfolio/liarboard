Project Documentation

Information regarding the use, privacy, and future of LiarBoard.

Instructions

How to use: Browse the Board on the home page. Use the search bar to find specific names, filter by Class to narrow results, or sort by "Most Debunked" and "Recently Corrected" to surface the most active fact-check entries.

Reading an Entry: Click any card to open its detail panel. Each entry has a Claim vs. Truth toggle — the red panel shows the public claim, the green panel shows the verified truth. Source links are listed where available.

Submissions: Use the Contact page to generate a formatted request. Copy the output and email it to our admin at liarboard.business@gmail.com. When requesting a removal, include who you are, your relation to the subject, and the reason for removal. Data corrections can also be requested by emailing the admin with the name, your identity, and the corrected data.

Note: All entries are reviewed manually before appearing or disappearing, so changes may take time. Donors receive priority for all requests. See "Support the Project" below.

Privacy & Terms

Privacy Policy: LiarBoard does not store your personal data or IP for marketing or any other purpose. Your public IP address is tracked live but not stored, solely to enforce lightweight rate limits. This means your IP is never accessible to anyone at LiarBoard or any affiliated entities. Rate-limit logic is managed by Cloudflare's network, which hosts this site among millions of others. We cannot make guarantees about Cloudflare's own data practices. We only store the public-profile data visible on the Board: names, bios, images, claims, truths, and sources. Removal requests are processed manually and timing will vary.

Terms of Service: By using this site, you agree not to submit entries with the specific intention of harassment. All entries you request are hereby declared your responsibility, and LiarBoard and any affiliated entities are voided from responsibility — whether legal, moral, ethical, social, or otherwise. Content you request, including classifications and supporting information, is your submission, not LiarBoard's. All entries must serve public interest only. We reserve the right to deny or remove any entry at our discretion.

Support the Project

Keeping LiarBoard running on Cloudflare costs time and resources. If you find the Board useful and want to support its growth, consider donating. We don't yet have a built-in donation system, so email the admin to arrange it. Sharing the site is also a great way to help if donating isn't for you.
